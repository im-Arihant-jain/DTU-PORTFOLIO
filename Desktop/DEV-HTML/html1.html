Delhi Technological University


DTU University is a non-affiliating teaching and research university. It was established in 1941 as Delhi Polytechnic, and DTU Delhi was founded in 1952. DTU is ranked 61 in the overall category by NIRF 2023. It is not only the mother institution of many national institutions, such as IIT Delhi, NSIT, and the Faculty of Management Studies (FMS), but it is also the second-oldest engineering college in the country. The university has been running for about 78 years now. DTU offers courses like BTech, MTech, PhD, and MBA. It contains 14 departments that offer scientific and technological education and research courses.
